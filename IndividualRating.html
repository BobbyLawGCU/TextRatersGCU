Consensual Assessment of Writing

For the first 3 questions, please think about what helps you understand what the text is about.

To what degree does the chosen content help you understand what the text says?

To what degree does the chosen structure help you understand what the text says?

To what degree does the chosen language help you understand what the text says?

For the next 3 questions, please think about the impression you have of the writer: is this someone who knows how to write in this context?

To what degree is the content appropriate for the situation and purposes outlined in the brief?

To what degree is the structure appropriate for the situation and purposes outlined in the brief?

To what degree is the language appropriate for the situation and purposes outlined in the brief?
